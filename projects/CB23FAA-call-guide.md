# CB23FAA - CLIENT CALL GUIDE
## Coollooks Barberz 23 - Questions to Ask

---

## 👋 OPENING

"Thanks for taking the time to chat! I'm excited to officially kick off your project. This call is just for me to understand your business better so I can deliver the best result. Should take about 15-20 minutes."

---

## 🏪 YOUR BUSINESS STORY

**Ask:** "Can you tell me the story behind Coollooks? How did it all start?"

**Then ask:**
- "How long have you been open?"
- "What's a typical day like for you?"
- "How many clients are you seeing these days?"

---

## 🎯 YOUR GOALS

**Ask:** "If we could wave a magic wand and your business was exactly where you wanted it to be 12 months from now, what would that look like?"

**Follow up:**
- "What's the #1 goal you want us to help with?"
- "Is it more bookings? More visibility? Something else?"
- "Where do you see this business in 2-3 years?"

---

## 😱 CHALLENGES

**Ask:** "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now?"

**Listen for:**
- Getting new clients?
- Getting repeat clients?
- Booking system issues?
- Time management?

---

## 📱 GOOGLE BUSINESS

**Say:** "We're going to optimize your Google Business profile - it's usually the first thing people see. I need to check a few things."

**Ask:**
- "Can you pull up your Google Business profile on your phone?" (or "I'll look it up")
- "Do you have lots of photos on there now?"
- "Are you getting calls or messages through Google?"

**Our recommendations to mention:**
- "We should enable walk-ins AND appointments on there"
- "Add detailed services - it helps you show up in Google"
- "We'll get professional photos up there"

**Ask about review link:**
- "Is this your review link? https://g.page/r/CSYgqs6JzfLqEBM/review"

---

## 💇 SERVICES

**Ask:** "Can you walk me through all your services?"

**Write down:**
- [ ] Men's haircuts - $?
- [ ] Kids cuts - $?
- [ ] Braiding - $?
- [ ] Beard trims - $?
- [ ] Shaves - $?
- [ ] Other?

**Then ask:**
- "Do you want to promote braiding as much as barber services? Or is it secondary?"
- "Do you want to push kids services more? It's often an underserved market."

---

## 📸 PHOTOS

**Say:** "Professional photos make a HUGE difference on Google and your website."

**Ask:** "When can we schedule a photo shoot? I'll come to your shop."

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Inside the shop
- [ ] Lighting/ambiance
- [ ] Tools (clippers, scissors)
- [ ] Work stations
- [ ] Any team members

**Ask:** "Any areas you'd prefer we don't photograph?"

---

## 📅 BOOKING & OPERATIONS

**Ask:**
- "Walk-ins vs appointments - what's your ratio?"
- "Who built your booking system?"
- **IMPORTANT:** "Can customers book WITHOUT creating an account?"

**If NO:** "Can we ask them to enable guest booking? It really helps conversion."

**Confirm:**
- Phone number: ____________
- Address/Suburb: ____________
- Hours: ____________

---

## 🏆 COMPETITORS

**Ask:** "Who are your main competitors? Other barbers in the area?"

**Then ask:**
- "What do they do really well?"
- "What do they do badly?" (This is YOUR opportunity!)

---

## 💰 BUDGET & TIMING

**Ask:**
- "What's your budget for this project?"
- "Any deadline you're working towards?"
- "When would you like to launch?"

---

## ❓ THEIR QUESTIONS

**Be ready to answer:**

**"How much will this cost?"**
→ "I'll put together a proposal after this call with all options."

**"How long will it take?"**
→ "Google Business is usually 1-2 weeks. Website depends on content."

**"What do I need to do?"**
→ "Mainly just provide photos, your service list, and be available for questions."

---

## 👋 CLOSING

"Summarize what we discussed:
- We'll optimize your Google Business
- Build you a website
- Create your visual identity

I'll send you a proposal by [DATE]. Any questions before we wrap up?"

"Thanks so much for your time!"
