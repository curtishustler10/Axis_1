# Agency Lead Gen Templates

## Cold Outreach (Direct Message)

**Option 1 - Problem-Focused:**
```
Hey {{name}},

I noticed {{company}} is doing {{specific thing}}.

We've helped agencies like {{similar company}} get {{result}} through {{method}}.

Quick question - what's your biggest challenge with {{area}} right now?

Cheers,
Curt
```

**Option 2 - Social Proof:**
```
Hey {{name}},

Saw your post about {{topic}}. Solid insights.

We helped {{similar company}} {{achieve result}} in {{timeframe}}.

Would you be open to a quick call to share what worked?

Best,
Curt
```

## Objection Responses

**"Too expensive"**
→ "Happy to discuss budget. What would need to happen for this to be a yes?"

**"Not right now"**
→ "Understand. When would be a better time? I'll follow up then."

**"Who are you?"**
→ "Sorry for the cold outreach. We help agencies with {{service}}. Thought it might be relevant."
